Matchmaker

Summary

Industries want to recruit students with real-world experience but it’s difficult for students to find opportunities to gain this experience. After an in-depth understanding of professor, industry, and student needs, I worked with the UCSD Design Lab and SAP to conceptualize a platform called Matchmaker which takes industry projects and matches them to project based courses enabling students to gain real-world experience while learning course concepts at the same time.

From our competitive analysis of websites and platforms that have databases of projects, we learned that there are currently no platforms that connect industry projects directly to professors and they don’t help facilitate negotiations.

Prototype

Our final solution is a platform called Matchmaker that holds a database of projects from industry that are matched to the preferences and needs of professors so that they can provide real-world learning experiences to their students in their project-based courses.
